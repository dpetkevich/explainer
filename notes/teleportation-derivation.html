Quantum teleportation — the full derivation

Companion note to the “A superposition can be moved, never copied” scene · arXiv 2603.28627 explainer

Setup

Memory qubit M holds the unknown state:

|ψ⟩M=α|0⟩+β|1⟩

Helpers H₁ and H₂ are prepared in the Bell state:

|Φ+⟩H1H2=12(|00⟩+|11⟩)

H₁ sits next to M; H₂ is at the processor. The joint state of all three qubits is the product |ψ⟩M⊗|Φ+⟩H1H2.

The two questions are two commuting joint observables

The Bell measurement on (M, H₁) is the simultaneous measurement of:

  • Z⊗Z — joint parity in the computational (0/1) basis: eigenvalue +1 on span{|00⟩,|11⟩} (“same”), −1 on span{|01⟩,|10⟩} (“different”). This is literally a stabilizer / parity-check measurement — the same primitive as syndrome extraction in error correction.
  • X⊗X — the identical parity question asked in the conjugate basis |±⟩=12(|0⟩±|1⟩): “in the wave (phase) language, are you two in step or out of step?”

These commute ([Z⊗Z,X⊗X]=0, since each anticommutes twice), so both can be measured together. Their four joint eigenspaces are exactly the four Bell states:

stateZ⊗ZX⊗Xrelationship
|Φ+⟩=12(|00⟩+|11⟩)+1+1identical
|Φ-⟩=12(|00⟩−|11⟩)+1−1phase-flipped
|Ψ+⟩=12(|01⟩+|10⟩)−1+1bit-flipped
|Ψ-⟩=12(|01⟩−|10⟩)−1−1both flips

Two ±1 outcomes = the two classical bits. Note what a Bell state is: a simultaneous eigenstate of <em>relational</em> operators only — definite correlations, maximally indefinite individuals. That is the formal content of “reveals relationships, never contents.”

The identity (the anagram)

Expand the initial state and regroup (M, H₁) in the Bell basis. Pure algebra — no dynamics, no measurement:

|ψ⟩M⊗|Φ+⟩H1H2=12[|Φ+⟩MH1⊗I|ψ⟩H2+|Φ-⟩MH1⊗Z|ψ⟩H2+|Ψ+⟩MH1⊗X|ψ⟩H2+|Ψ-⟩MH1⊗XZ|ψ⟩H2]

where X (bit flip) and Z (phase flip) are the Pauli operators. This identity holds <strong>before anything is measured</strong>: the exact three-qubit state already decomposes as “a Bell label on (M, H₁), correlated with H₂ carrying U|ψ⟩, U∈I,Z,X,XZ.” That is the rigorous version of “the state is already at the destination in four disguises.”

Sanity check — trace out M and H₁. H₂'s reduced density matrix is

ρH2=14ΣUU|ψ⟩⟨ψ|U†=I2

— maximally mixed. Locally, H₂ carries <em>no</em> information about ψ until the classical bits arrive: the no-signaling guarantee. Nothing beats light.

The measurement

Measuring Z⊗Z and X⊗X projects (M, H₁) onto one Bell state. From the identity, each outcome has Born probability |12|2=14, independent of α and β — formally why the two bits leak nothing about ψ. Post-measurement:

  • (M, H₁) collapse to the observed Bell state, which contains no trace of α, β. The original is gone — no-cloning respected by the algebra, not by fiat.
  • H₂ collapses to the correlated branch U|ψ⟩, with U determined by the outcome.

“Forcing” is now precise: the initial state is not an eigenstate of Z⊗Z or X⊗X, so those relational quantities have <em>no value</em> before measurement; projection creates one, sampled by the Born rule.

The correction

The two bits identify U; the processor applies U† (a single Pauli: I, Z, X, or ZX), leaving H₂ in exactly α|0⟩+β|1⟩.

In hardware the Bell measurement compiles to CNOT → Hadamard → two single-qubit Z measurements. In this paper's machinery it is performed at the logical level by code surgery: the ancilla patch measures the joint logical operators barZbarZ and barXbarX, repeated ≈ 2d/3 rounds for fault tolerance — which is why the fetch's “16 rounds” scene and this derivation are the same physics.

One-sentence summary

The tensor-product structure lets the same global state be written with ψ's amplitudes attached to H₂ in four Pauli frames; a Bell measurement on (M, H₁) collapses the frame label, and two classical bits plus one Pauli rotation fix the frame.
